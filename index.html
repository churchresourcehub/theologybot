How to read this

This is a load-bearing map, not an encyclopedia. A few foundation claims sit at the bottom and everything above hangs from them by a visible line. The shape follows Wesley's way of salvation and lets one locus lead into the next in the spirit of Hodgson and King. Select any node to begin, or switch to Ask to question the corpus. Every answer is grounded in his own pages and quotes.

Ask about his theology

Answers draw on the full text of everything: his sermons, Pastor's Notes, Bible studies, academic and ordination papers, and the practical church-resource documents his church runs on. Ask a doctrine question or a "how do we run this" question and it answers with the full context, naming its sources. If nothing covers your question, it says so rather than guess.
